What We Do

Electrical Services You Can Count On

Quick outlet fix or a whole-house rewire, you get the same straight talk and clean work either way.

Residential Wiring & Rewires

If your house was built before the '80s, the wiring has probably seen better days. We rewire older homes all the time and do it to current code.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Running out of breaker slots or lights flicker when the AC kicks on? A panel upgrade usually sorts it. We handle the permit too.

Outlet & Switch Installation

Two-prong outlets, dead receptacles, a kitchen running on one shared circuit. We sort it out and make sure it's up to code.

Lighting Installation

Recessed cans, ceiling fans, under-cabinet strips, outdoor floods. We run the wiring and do the full install, not just the fixture swap.

EV Charger Installation

Got a new EV and need a Level 2 charger at home? We run the dedicated circuit, mount the unit, pull the permit. Usually done in a day.

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Something stopped working and you're not sure why? We track down the problem, explain it in plain terms, and fix it.

About Us

Local Work. Personal Service.
Done Right.

Burleson's Northwest Electric has been serving Olympia and Thurston County for over 20 years. Owner Michael Burleson built this company on one idea: treat every home like your own and do the work right the first time.

Michael is a licensed master electrician with hands-on experience in everything from older craftsman homes to new construction. He shows up on time, gives honest assessments, and never recommends work that isn't needed.

We're fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We pull permits when required so your work is done safely and to code. When you call Burleson's, you get Michael.
